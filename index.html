Hello! We are FTC 11138 Robo Eclipse, a 8th year veteran robotics team based in Bellevue, WA, USA. We are composed of 8 students in grades 8-12.




What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global program that aims to encourage students to be more involved in the STEM fields. Not only do students in the FIRST program learn how to learn and program robots, they also learn lifetime skills such as Gracious Professionalism and communication skills. FIRST is also the world's leading youth-serving nonprofit advancing STEM education. In total, FIRST has four different programs for different age groups, including FIRST Lego League, FIRST Technology Challenge and FIRST Robotics Competition.
