About Us


Our team is comprised of 10-students who participate in FIRST Tech Challenge. We are based in Bellevue, Washington, and our ages range from grade 8 all the way to grade 12, and we are from several school districts, including Lake Washington, Bellevue, and Issaquah School Districts.
